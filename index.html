Sri Lankan Gods

Sri Lanka has many gods which people worship for good health, wealth and well being.

God Kataragama

Kataragama is one of the most important gods in Sri Lanka. He is worshipped by Buddhists, Hindus, and people of other faiths.

Goddess Pattini

Pattini is the goddess of fertility and health. Many women pray to her for protection during childbirth and for family well-being.

Goddess Kali

Kali is known as a powerful goddess who removes evil and protects devotees from harm.
